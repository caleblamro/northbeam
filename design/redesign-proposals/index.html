Design exploration · July 2026

Page redesign proposals

Nine layout concepts for the three highest-traffic surfaces. All of them keep the existing token system — near-black brand, single indigo accent, Inter, hairline borders — and only rework composition, hierarchy, and density. Same seed data everywhere so comparisons are fair.

Use the sun/moon toggle in the topbar of any mockup — every concept is themed for light and dark.

Home dashboard

currently: hero card → stat strip → two cards

Today everything is boxed and equally weighted, and the actionable "needs attention" panel is bottom-right where it's seen last.

H1Editorial ledger Kills the boxes. Pipeline number sits directly on the page at display size, metrics become a hairline band with micro-sparklines, activity is a flat ledger with a mono time gutter. Fixes: flat hierarchy — typography does the work instead of card borders. Open mockup → H2Command center Dense three-column cockpit: stat tiles + pipeline-by-stage bars, a real trend chart with period switcher, and a unified inbox merging attention items with activity. One viewport, no scroll. Fixes: low information density; adds stage breakdown and closing-soon table the current page lacks. Open mockup → H3Focus queue Task-first morning view: greeting, slim stat band, then needs-attention promoted to full-width actionable rows with one-click actions ("Send proposal", "Log a touch"). Fixes: the actionable panel being buried; the page tells you what to do, not just what exists. Open mockup →
